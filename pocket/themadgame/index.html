yo, welcome to my little corner of the internet! i'm pollyanna (but just call me polly), and this blog? it's basically my personal stash of random stuff (yeah, i'm pretty sure you can tell i'm into goblincore). everything you see here is built by yours truly (okay, and maybe a little help from some cool code i found online...don't tell anyone)

i'm not great at intros, but here goes: i'm a dreamer with a bunch of ideas, and if you're reading this, you probably wanna know a little about me. so, deal with it, i guess?

i'm an artist, a grunge fashion lover, and a total tech nerd who spends waaaay too much time modding old consoles and messing around with emulators. sometimes i feel like i should've been born in the 90s (except, ya know, the whole "no internet" thing)

when i'm not doodling or spray-painting some old wall (for art, not vandalism... mostly), i'm jamming out to punk music or messing around with tech that's older than me

oh, i also love binge-watching movies. seriously, i'm obsessed... (please ask me about my favorite directors!!)

i'll totally not gonna judge your taste in movies (ok, maybe just a little)

you could say i'm "politically intense" i like thinking about how things could be better, and how we all have a role in making the world suck a little less. i'm all about being yourself, standing up for what you believe in, and just generally not letting anyone tell you who you are. life's too short for that crap

so as you can notice from this unnecessary messy bio, this blog is kinda just my brain thrown up onto a webpage. expect random thoughts, art dumps, tech stuff, movie rants, and probably a few rants about how capitalism totally sucks ('cause it does)

thanks for stopping by! feel free to look around or drop a comment if you vibe with anything. unless you're a fascist? then you can FUCK OFF

polly out ✌️
