About the Project

Deltarune: The Metropolis is a DELTARUNE Chapter 3 AU created by HUECYCLES, Lisspeed and a talented team. It consists of a compilation of mockups, art, music and "gameplay footage" featuring our take on a Chapter 3/TV Dark World! Lots of original characters, NPCs and interpretations of the DELTARUNE cast will be featured here.

It is currently unknown if there will be a fangame or not, but we hope this collection of creative works will interest you in some way!
